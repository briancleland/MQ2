Welcome to the Newry and MourneQuest.

I am Cobs the Clurichaun and I am in need of your help. The dreaded Shimnavore has captured my dearest friend Jack Turner and somehow, they have travelled through time all the way here to the City of the Yew - I believe you call it "Newry". But the trail has grown cold.

I know Jack and I am sure he has left a trail through your wonderful city. I have a map but I am unsure where it leads. Will you join me on a journey and unlock the hidden secrets Newry holds?
